# Internship vs Courses: What Really Gets You Hired?

When building your Data Science career, you face a choice: focus on courses or seek internships? The answer might surprise you.

## The Course Route

### Pros
- Structured learning
- Certificates and credentials
- Foundation knowledge
- Flexible timing

### Cons
- Limited real-world application
- May lack practical experience
- Employers value experience over certificates

## The Internship Route

### Pros
- Real-world experience
- Industry exposure
- Networking opportunities
- Practical problem-solving
- Strong resume builder

### Cons
- May require existing skills
- Competitive to secure
- Time commitment

## The Winning Combination

The best approach combines both:
1. **Start with Courses**: Build your foundation
2. **Apply Through Projects**: Practice what you learn
3. **Seek Internships**: Gain real-world experience
4. **Continue Learning**: Stay updated with new courses

## What Employers Actually Want

According to hiring managers, they prioritize:
1. **Practical Experience** (60%)
2. **Problem-Solving Skills** (25%)
3. **Certificates/Courses** (15%)

## Conclusion

While courses provide the foundation, internships and projects demonstrate your ability to apply knowledge. The combination is what truly gets you hired.
